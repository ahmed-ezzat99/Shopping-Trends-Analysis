# Business Questions Answers

## 1. Sales Performance by Category
The Clothing category is the top-performing category, generating $294K in total sales.

## 2. Top Products
Dresses are the highest revenue-generating individual product.

## 3. Gender and Size Analysis
Male customers generate the majority of revenue, particularly sizes M ($208K) and L ($126K).

## 4. Age Group Contribution
Customers aged 40+ are the most valuable segment, contributing $385.2K in total revenue.

## 5. Regional Performance
Montana ranks as the top-performing state with $16.3K in sales, followed by California and Idaho.

## 6. Shipping Impact
Free Shipping has a strong positive impact on sales, contributing $115K in revenue.

## 7. Payment Methods
Credit Cards are the most frequently used payment method across all customer segments.
